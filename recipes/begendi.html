How to prepare a delicious begendi

Description

This mouth-watering dish has a ton of history behind it and it's really easy to prepare. The main ingredients are eggplants and cheese.

You have the option of either preparing the dish as a stand-alone full meal together with diced beef and tomato sauce or as a side-dish. In this recipe, we'll prepare the side-dish version.

Ingredients

  • Grilled eggplant
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Add flour
  3. Heat until the mixture is a brownish colour
  4. Add the eggplants
  5. Add milk
  6. Finally, add the cheese and season to your liking
